How To Play

Your goal is to guess and craft the secret item from the ingredients given in your inventory:

-You have 10 guesses

-If your guess is incorrect, some of the grid may be coloured to give you feedback :)

-A green square means the ingredient placed on that square is correct and in the right spot!

-An orange square means that this ingredient is needed somewhere else on the grid, but not at the spot...

-If the square remains grey, the ingredient you placed here is not required, hmmm

Click below to start the game and test your true knowledge of Minecraft...Your reputation is on the line!
