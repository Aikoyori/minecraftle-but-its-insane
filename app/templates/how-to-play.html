How To Play

Your goal is to try to craft the secret item from the ingredients in your inventory just like Normal Minecraftle but:

  1. You have 20 guesses
  2. You are given every items that are considered "ingredients" (you can craft things with it)
  3. If your guess is incorrect, some of the grid may be coloured to give you feedback
  4. If the square remains grey, the ingredient you placed here is not in the recipe, hmmm...
  5. An orange square means that this ingredient is needed somewhere else on the grid, but not at the spot...
  6. A green square means the ingredient placed on that square is correct and in the right spot!
